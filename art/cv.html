Wang Shan 王珊

I am a Master's student at Goldsmiths, University of London, majoring in MA Virtual and Augmented Reality. I am a Creative Technologist and VR Developer interested in creating novel XR experiences that unveil the past, the future, and the invisible dimensions of our reality.

Before joining Goldsmiths in 2025, I studied at the USC-SJTU Institute of Cultural and Creative Industry, Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

I received my B.S. degree in Computer Science from Nanjing University in 2024. I worked as a Research Assistant at the Institute of Neuroscience, Chinese Academy of Sciences (2023) and was a visiting student at the National Taiwan University of Arts (2023).

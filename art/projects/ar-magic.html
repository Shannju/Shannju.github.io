AR Magic: Frostbound Eve

MR Experience with Meta AR/MR Technology

// Role

Project Leader / Main Programmer

// Technology

Meta AR/MR, ISDK Hand Tracking, Unity VFX Graph

// Timeline

2025

// Project Overview

2025

"AR Magic: Frostbound Eve" — An immersive mixed reality experience that bridges the gap between physical environments and fantasy narratives.

This MR experience utilizes Meta AR/MR technology to transform domestic spaces into dynamic sites of interactive storytelling. By implementing Destructible Global Mesh effects, players cast spells to physically "shatter" real-world walls, revealing hidden, stylized environments beneath. The project integrates Meta ISDK Hand Tracking for gesture-based spellcasting and Unity VFX Graph for immersive particle systems, creating a seamless blend of reality and fantasy.

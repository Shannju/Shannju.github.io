BALANCE

A Meditative VR Sensory Study

// Project Overview

2026

BALANCE — A meditative VR sensory study in vestibular-visual equilibrium and body-as-interface.

Built by Shine Wang, the experience uses 6DoF roll tracking, non-linear balance mapping, and stillness detection to transform physical calm into realtime visual and spatial-audio feedback.

System Requirements

Minimum: Quest 2 / Android 10+ / 6GB RAM / 1GB Storage

Recommended: Quest 3 / Headphones / Clear play area

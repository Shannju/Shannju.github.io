"Black Sheep" — A VR psychological narrative game exploring dreams and the subconscious.

The interactive experience, Black Sheep, was produced by students Shine Wang and Minna Yang at Shanghai Jiao Tong University’s Institute for Cultural and Creative Industry. The project explores the intersection of psychoanalysis and immersive media, and was featured in the Future Vision: China-UK Youth Digital Creative Exhibition (2025, Shanghai).

This work constructs a “Room Between Dream and Reality,” where the environment shifts dynamically based on player actions. Using spatial sound and multi-sensory storytelling, players manipulate symbolic dream objects to uncover suppressed memories, transforming abstract psychological states into a tangible, immersive journey through the subconscious mind.

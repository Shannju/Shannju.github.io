// Project Overview

2025

"The Quest: Vacheron Constantin 270th Anniversary" — An immersive retrospective at Zhangyuan, Shanghai, held from July 7 to July 20, 2025.

Held at the historic Zhangyuan, this seven-chapter narrative exhibition commemorates 270 years of Vacheron Constantin's technical and artistic legacy. The showcase featured the Berkley Pocket Watch—the world's most complicated timepiece—alongside a series of masterworks honoring global civilizations.

Images: SocialBeta

// Technical Execution

As the on-site technical lead, I managed the maintenance and hardware-software synchronization of 10+ interactive installations. My work centered on real-time debugging and the optimization of the "Stellar Chart" immersive space. By coordinating complex AV signals with mechanical display states—mapped specifically to Shanghai's geographic coordinates—the installation successfully bridges heritage horology with a new horizon of digital craftsmanship.
